## What this source covers

Evaluation of hosting options for the [[Gear Horse]] marketing site, given requirements for a custom domain, marketing posts, product page, signup forms, and UTM parameter forwarding to [[Carrd Make Notion Stack]].

---

## Key takeaways

### Options evaluated

| Option | Cost | Fit |
|---|---|---|
| Framer | ~$5/mo | Best single-platform fit |
| Webflow | $14/mo | Overkill, expensive |
| Carrd Pro + Ghost/Beehiiv | $19/yr + free tier | Lean; leverages existing stack |
| Astro + Cloudflare Pages | Free | Zero cost, but dev effort required |

### Recommendation

- **Pre-Kickstarter (current phase):** Stay on Carrd Pro Lite. No blog needed before launch.
- **If marketing posts are needed:** Move to Framer (~$5/mo) as the lowest-friction single-platform path.
- **Zero-cost ceiling:** Astro + Cloudflare Pages, but requires developer setup.

### Decision dependency
The hosting choice hinges on one question: is a blog/marketing post section needed before Kickstarter? If not, no upgrade is warranted. This connects to [[Zero-Capital Launch Strategy]] — avoid spending until validation is complete.

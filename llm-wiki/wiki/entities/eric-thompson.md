# Eric Thompson

Co-Founder and Brand Lead at [[Gear Horse]].

---

## Role

Leads brand identity, content strategy, and business development for Gear Horse. Responsible for the founding narrative, voice, and go-to-market strategy.

---

## Background

Based in Oregon. Part of a founding group that has used a Deschutes National Forest campsite for approximately 6 years. The Gear Horse idea emerged at that site over beers (Deschutes IPA) and a campfire.

---

## Contributions documented

- Defined brand voice: conversational, self-deprecating, Oregon-specific
- Designed the [[Zero-Capital Launch Strategy]]
- Led naming process (Gear Horse selected; several alternatives researched and rejected as taken)
- Authored founding context document
- Set up and documented [[Carrd Make Notion Stack]]

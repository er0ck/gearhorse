# Chair Armor

Model-specific camp chair covers by [[Gear Horse]]. Highest gross margin of the three founding products.

---

## Problem it solves

No US-made, model-specific chair covers exist at any price point. Premium camp chairs (Helinox, YETI, etc.) degrade from UV exposure, moisture, and storage without protection.

---

## Specs

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Material | Waxed canvas or 500D Cordura |
| Manufacturing | Sewn in USA |
| Closure | Drawstring + buckle |
| Fit | Model-specific and universal |
| Retail price target | $38–$54 |
| Gross margin target | 60–70% |

---

## Manufacturing path

Cut-and-sew manufacturers in NC, SC, GA via ThomasNet ("cut and sew manufacturer USA").

---

## Name origin

The name "Chair Armor" received unanimous finger guns from the founding group — the highest honor in the Gear Horse naming process.

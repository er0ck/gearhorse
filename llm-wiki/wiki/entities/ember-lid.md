# Ember Lid

Folding cast-iron campfire pizza lid by [[Gear Horse]].

---

## Problem it solves

No foldable cast-iron campfire dome exists. Current workaround: foil crimped over a pan. The Ember Lid replaces that with a proper hinged cast-iron dome for campfire pizza and other cooking.

---

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Material | Cast iron |

| Folded size | ~12"×12"×1.5" |

US cast-iron foundries via ThomasNet ("gray iron casting manufacturer"). Prototype castings first before production run.
